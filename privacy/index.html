Privacy Policy

Simmer: Smart Cooking

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

Overview

Simmer: Smart Cooking ("Simmer", "we", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

Information We Collect

When you use Simmer, we may collect the following information to provide and improve our services: account information (name, email address) when you sign in with Apple; household profile data including dietary preferences, allergies, and nutrition goals you provide; meal and nutrition data you log or scan within the app; pantry items you add manually or via receipt scanning; and app usage data such as feature interactions and error reports.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to personalize meal plans and recipe recommendations based on your preferences; provide calorie scanning and nutrition tracking; sync your data across devices; improve app performance and fix bugs; and communicate with you about your account or support requests.

Camera and Photo Library

Simmer requests camera access to scan meals for calorie estimation and photo library access to select meal photos or grocery receipts. Photos and camera data are processed to provide nutritional analysis and are not shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data with service providers who help us operate the app (such as cloud hosting and authentication services), but only as necessary to provide our services. We may also share aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and hosted on trusted cloud infrastructure. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion.

Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us at support@cookwithsimmer.com. You can also delete your account directly within the app under Settings.

Children's Privacy

Simmer is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of Simmer after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@cookwithsimmer.com.
